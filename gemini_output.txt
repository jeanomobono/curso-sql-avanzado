✦ The vuelos schema contains a highly dense dataset focused on flight operations and reservations concentrated in a short, specific period in early 2026.

  Dataset Overview
   * Total Flights: 57,971 records.
   * Total Reservations: 172,324 records.
   * Total Clients: 9,999 records.
   * Total Seats (Plazas): 2,898,550 records.
   * Temporal Scope: Flights are scheduled daily between February 22, 2026, and March 18, 2026.

  Key Entities & Structure
   * Core Operations (VUELOS & RESERVAS): The schema tracks flights, their dates, origin/destination airports (34 total), airline companies, aircraft capacity, and
     catering services. Reservations link clients to specific seats (PLAZAS) on these flights.
   * Passenger Demographics (CLIENTES): Detailed information is available for nearly 10,000 clients, including geographic data (City/Country) and demographic attributes
     like sex, age range, marital status, and employment status.
   * Sales Channels (AGENCIAS & EMPRESAS_VIAJES): Reservations are associated with travel agencies and travel companies.
   * Lookup Tables: The schema is well-normalized with lookup tables for flight types (e.g., Regional, National, Charter, International), catering options, and
     reservation types.

  This dataset is particularly rich for analyzing airline performance, passenger demographics, and booking density during a peak travel period.
